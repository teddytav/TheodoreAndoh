About Me


I am a skilled PowerApps developer with 1.5 years of experience in designing innovative solutions to optimize business processes. My expertise lies in Microsoft Power tools, Python, and C/C++ programming. Over the course of my career, I have successfully developed various applications, including visitor management systems, quality assurance tools, and employee tracking platforms. Currently, I work as a Microsoft PowerApps Developer at Ohly, where I create custom connectors, automate workflows, and develop apps for finance and operational tracking. Previously, during my internship at KWS Berlin GmbH, I built apps for booking, quality control, and employee engagement. My professional experience also includes a role in quality assurance at Nestlé, where I utilized SAP and Excel for data analysis.

In addition to my technical expertise, I am fluent in English (C1) and proficient in German (B1). My soft skills include leadership, communication, and teamwork. Outside of work, I am a passionate sports enthusiast, actively participating in Judo and holding a black belt in Taekwondo.
